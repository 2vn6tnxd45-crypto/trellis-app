Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 18, 2025

At Krib ("we," "our," or "us"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our property management application (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you use Krib:

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your email address and password. If you use third-party login services (Google, Apple), we collect the identifiers provided by those services.
  • Property Data: We collect the property addresses, system details (HVAC, plumbing, etc.), maintenance records, and photos you upload to your Home Log.
  • Contractor Submissions: If you use our Contractor Request feature, we collect the data submitted by third parties via the links you generate.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the Krib Service.
  • To allow you to access, manage, and update your property records.
  • To generate reports (such as the Property Pedigree) at your request.
  • To communicate with you about updates, security alerts, and support messages.

3. Data Ownership & Transfer

Your Data is Yours. Krib claims no ownership over the specific property records you create. The primary value of our Service is to create a transferable digital record. You may choose to transfer your account and all associated property data to a new owner upon the sale of your home.

4. Advertising & Data Sharing

To keep Krib free and useful, we may share information in specific ways to provide relevant home services and offers.

  • Personalized Offers: We may use non-sensitive, aggregated property attributes (e.g., "Pool Owner", "AC Age > 10 Years") to display relevant advertisements or sponsored recommendations (e.g., "Pool Cleaning Service").
  • Service Providers: We share data with third-party vendors (like Google Cloud or Firebase) who perform essential infrastructure services on our behalf, such as hosting and database management.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g. a court order).
  • No Direct Sale of Contact Info: We do not sell your email address or phone number directly to third parties without your explicit consent (for example, if you specifically request a quote from a contractor through our "Pro Connect" feature).
  • Opt-Out: You may manage your preferences for personalized advertising within your account settings.

5. Security of Your Information

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information. This includes encryption of data in transit and at rest via our cloud providers. However, no electronic transmission or storage system is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Your Privacy Rights (CCPA/GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Know what personal data we collect
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of sale of personal information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

To exercise these rights: Email privacy@mykrib.app
